About Us

Founded in 2016 Sevastopol IT Company SolarLab focuses on customized and proprietary software development.

SolarLab helps businesses to operate efficiently and transparently providing unique solutions in public procurements and fintech areas.

The majority of our projects is based on Microsoft technologies (as many of our developers are Microsoft certified); in addition to that, our deep expertise in web, mobile and data management systems turns IT into our strategic asset. We believe in constant improvement, that is why we have been successful in machine learning, natural language interfaces, and blockchain technologies, which have enabled us to acquire significant experience with Mutlichain, Ethereum and Masterchain.

Over time, we have established strong and lasting relationships with Microsoft - nowadays Microsoft Silver and Gold Competencies confirm our extensive knowledge and outstanding experience. Our effort is to tap into powerful next-generation IT solutions and extensive partner relationships, for this reason we are actively cooperating with Sevastopol University and recruiting specialists from all over the Russian Federation. Today SolarLab has over 85 employees working for its clients.

At SolarLab every associate makes a difference. We create the opportunity for anyone interested to learn new skills, apply expertise, and solve complex technical tasks while studying in our own IT Academy. After graduation, the best developers and QA engineers become part of our team.

In December 2017 inspired by the need to be flexible in challenging economic times and the desire to adjust our operations to business needs and trends, SolarLab joined the Russian Association of Cryptocurrencies and Blockchain.
